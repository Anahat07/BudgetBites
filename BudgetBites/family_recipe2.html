STUFFED BELL PEPPER [serves 4-6]

Cook Time: 20 minutes

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Wash, dry and cut each bell pepper in half horizontally
  3. Remove the stem from the top half of each bell pepper
  4. Season how you would like and bake for 20 minutes
  5. While the bell peppers are baking, make the filling. Heat a large pan over medium heat and saute the broccoli and scrambled eggs together.
  6. Next add 1 cup of uncooked rice and the smashed tomatoes
  7. Place a lid on the pan, turn the heat up to medium-high, and allow the mixture to come to a full boil. After 20 minutes, turn the heat off and let it rest for 5 minutes
  8. Begin to stuff as much filling as you can into each bell pepper, filling them all the way to the top.
  9. Top each bell pepper evenly with the cheese.
  10. Optional: Broil for 5 minutes
